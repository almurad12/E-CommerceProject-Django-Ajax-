Privacy Policy

The Art & Craft - CrystaL values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website www.thecrystalcraft.com, interact with us on social media, or visit our retail store.

By accessing our services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect
  • Personal details: Name, email, phone number, shipping/billing address.
  • Order information: Products purchased, payment method, and delivery details.
  • Communication data: Messages via email, social media (Facebook/Instagram), WhatsApp, or phone.
  • Technical data: IP address, device/browser type, pages visited (via cookies or analytics).

We only collect the data necessary to process your orders, deliver services, and improve your shopping experience.

2. How We Use Your Data
  • Processing and delivering your orders.
  • Sending order confirmations, invoices, and shipping updates.
  • Responding to your inquiries via email, phone, or social media.
  • Improving our website, marketing, and product offerings.
  • Sending newsletters or promotions (if subscribed).
3. Sharing of Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share it with trusted third parties for operational purposes, such as:

  • Delivery partners – for shipping and delivery updates.
  • Payment providers – bKash, Nagad, bank, or card processors.
  • IT and hosting providers – to ensure website functionality.

All third parties are required to protect your information and only use it for the intended purpose.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies to provide a smooth browsing experience and to understand visitor behavior. Cookies may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., which pages you visited).

5. Marketing & Communication
  • If you subscribe to our newsletter, we may send updates about products, sales, and promotions. You can unsubscribe anytime.
  • We may use Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, or similar tools to improve advertising. These may collect anonymous browsing behavior.
6. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and limited staff access to protect your information. However, no online transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

7. Your Rights
  • Access and request a copy of your personal data.
  • Request corrections if your data is inaccurate.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
8. Data Retention

We store your information only as long as necessary for:

  • Fulfilling orders and warranties.
  • Legal and accounting requirements.
  • Marketing purposes (until you unsubscribe).
9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites (e.g., courier tracking, payment gateways). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Updates to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.
